===============================
SYNTHETIC BANK LOAN POLICY GUIDE
Version: 1.0
===============================

1. INTRODUCTION
This document outlines the synthetic loan approval policies used for training and testing AI systems. These rules are not based on any real financial institution.

--------------------------------------------------

2. LOAN TYPES

2.1 Personal Loan
- Purpose: Medical expenses, travel, education, emergencies
- Loan Amount: $1,000 – $50,000
- Tenure: 1 – 5 years

2.2 Mortgage Loan
- Purpose: Purchase or refinance residential property
- Loan Amount: $50,000 – $1,000,000
- Tenure: 10 – 30 years

2.3 Auto Loan
- Purpose: Purchase of new or used vehicles
- Loan Amount: $5,000 – $100,000
- Tenure: 2 – 7 years

--------------------------------------------------

3. HARD HURDLES (MANDATORY CRITERIA)

The applicant must satisfy ALL of the following:

- Minimum credit score: 650
- No record of bankruptcy in the last 5 years
- Applicant must be at least 21 years old
- Must have a stable source of income
- No ongoing loan defaults

If any of the above conditions are not met, the loan must be automatically rejected.

--------------------------------------------------

4. SOFT HURDLES (PREFERRED CRITERIA)

These factors influence approval likelihood and loan terms:

- Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio should be under 40%
- Employment stability of at least 12 months preferred
- Higher credit score (>700) increases approval chances
- Savings or asset ownership improves applicant profile
- Existing relationship with the bank is a positive factor

Applications not meeting these criteria may still be approved with stricter terms.

--------------------------------------------------

5. DOCUMENT CHECKLIST

Applicants must provide the following documents:

5.1 Identity Proof (any one)
- Passport
- Driver’s License
- National ID Card

5.2 Income Proof
- Last 3 months' pay stubs
- Latest tax return (1–2 years)
- Bank statements (last 6 months)

5.3 Address Proof
- Utility bills
- Rental agreement
- Government-issued ID with address

5.4 Loan-Specific Documents

For Mortgage Loans:
- Property documents
- Sale agreement

For Auto Loans:
- Vehicle quotation/invoice
- Insurance details

--------------------------------------------------

6. DECISION RULE SUMMARY

- Reject if any hard hurdle fails
- Approve if all hard hurdles pass and most soft hurdles are met
- Conditional approval if soft hurdles are partially met

--------------------------------------------------
